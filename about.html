Our Story

Where It All Started

Starting a business in 2020 was a challenge few dared to take. For us, it was the culmination of a lifelong dream to bring authentic, high-quality coffee and a warm, neighborhood vibe to Ambernath.

What began as a small passion project has grown into a community hub where friendships are forged over steaming mugs of lattes and artisanal brunches.

"We don't just serve food — we serve memories."
